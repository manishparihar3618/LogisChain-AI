# Project Overview

LogisChain AI is a Machine Learning project that combines Supply Chain Analytics and Financial Risk Analytics.
The system analyzes logistics operations such as shipments, inventory, suppliers, and transportation to predict future business and financial risks.
Problem Statement
Many companies face financial losses because of supply chain disruptions.

## Examples

Shipment delays
Port congestion
Supplier failures
Inventory shortages
Increased transportation costs
These operational problems eventually lead to financial problems such as reduced sales, cash flow issues, and credit risk.
The goal is to detect these risks early using Machine Learning.

## Main Objective

Build an AI-powered system that predicts financial and business risks using logistics and supply chain data.
The model should identify potential problems before they become major financial losses.
Real-World Example
Port Congestion
↓
Shipment Delay
↓
Low Inventory
↓
Sales Decrease
↓
Financial Risk Increase

LogisChain AI learns these relationships and predicts future risk levels.

## Main Aim

LogisChain AI predicts future financial and business risks by analyzing supply chain operations. The system bridges logistics data and financial analytics, helping organizations identify financial problems before they appear in traditional financial reports
